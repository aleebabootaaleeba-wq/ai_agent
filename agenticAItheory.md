Question 1: What is Agentic AI?
Answer:Agentic AI is a type of artifical intelligence that can make decisions and take actions on its own to achive a goal. It can plan steps, use tools, and interact with real-world syatems without constant human guidance.
Questiuon 2: How Agentic AI different from a normal chatbot?
Answer: A normal chatbot only replies to user messages. Agentic AI can: -Decide what to do next,-use tools or APIS, -Perform tasks automatically, -Work toward a goal step by step.
Question 3: Write one real-life example where Agentic AI can help humans?
Answer: A smart travel assistant: It can check flight prices, book tickets, reserve hotels, and send reminders automatically without asking the user at every step.
